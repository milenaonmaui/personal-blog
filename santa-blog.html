Santa Arrives on a Canoe

Posted on December 24, 2020

By Milena Kari

Santa and and his raindeers/paddlers arrived by Hawaiian Outrigger Canoe at Wailea Beach infront of the Grand Wailea Hotel. This has been a tradition for many years on Christmas Eve and an opportuniy for children and families to take photos with Santa. this year due to the big and dangerous shorebreak Santa had to just wave from distance and head beack to a safer beach landing spot.
